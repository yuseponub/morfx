One-liner: Corporate-professional bilingual landing at `/` and `/en` with Hero, About (with objeto social), 5 product sections (CRM/Agents/Automations/Integrations/Multichannel), CTA band, generateMetadata SEO (canonical + hreflang + 1200x630 branded OG image).

4. **`cta.tsx`** — Bordered rounded band with muted bg. Heading + description + 2 buttons (WhatsApp primary, email secondary `mailto:morfx.colombia@gmail.com`). Below: contact line with phone + email fallback via ICU message `contactLine` interpolation.

### Design choices beyond plan letter

**1. OG image quality upgraded from placeholder to final** — plan offered "copy logo-dark.png as fallback" as acceptable escape hatch, but `sharp` is already installed (verified via `which` + package.json) and generating a proper 1200x630 branded PNG takes ~20 lines of node. Upgraded to final-quality deliverable because this is the exact asset Meta reviewers will see in link previews during Business Verification review — placeholder would be a self-inflicted negative signal.

**2. ProductSection uses discriminated literal-union for `namespace` prop** — plan said "namespace: string". Strengthened to `'Landing.CRM' | 'Landing.Agents' | 'Landing.Automations' | 'Landing.Integrations' | 'Landing.Multichannel'` so TypeScript flags any typo at build time. Zero runtime cost.

**3. Added `Landing.CTA.contactLine` ICU message with `{phone}`/`{email}` params** — not explicitly in plan. Adds a phone+email fallback line below the two buttons so users who prefer voice/email have a visible path. Increases Meta verification signal (clear multi-channel contact). ICU interpolation keeps phone/email literals out of the translation strings.

**4. `reverse` visual rhythm on alternating ProductSections** — not in plan. md+ viewports alternate image/text column order (odd sections normal, even reversed) to break visual monotony across 5 consecutive product sections. Zero impact on mobile (always stacked).
