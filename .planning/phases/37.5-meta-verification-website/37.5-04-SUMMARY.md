## Decisions Made

- **Nested Finalidades subsection instead of flat 7.2.1:** Source doc section 7.2 contains two distinct bullet lists (data categories + processing purposes) separated by an intro paragraph. JSON schema only supports single bullets[] per subsection. Rather than inflate subsection count to 9 (violating plan's `length=8` requirement) OR merge both lists (losing semantic distinction), used nested subsections[] with a sub-heading "Finalidades del Tratamiento". This motivated extending LegalSection to recursive subsections during Task 4.
- **h4 + left-border styling for level-1 nested subsections:** Differentiates from h3 (primary subsection) without creating typographic noise. Mirrors the visual hierarchy of printed legal documents.
- **SECTION_KEYS constant in each page:** Single source of truth drives both TOC navigation and content rendering. Impossible for TOC to drift out of sync with rendered content. Adding a new section requires one edit to the constant array + the JSON file.
- **t.raw() over structured translation helpers:** Legal content is data-heavy (paragraphs[], bullets[], nested subsections) and must match the JSON shape verbatim. t.raw() sidesteps next-intl's message-format runtime overhead and keeps the component free of per-field lookups.
- **Privacy contact section has full legal address + phone; Terms contact section stays short:** Privacy contact is the ARCO channel (data-subject rights under Ley 1581 art. 9, 14). Regulator-facing content benefits from maximal specificity. Terms section 15 is the general contact channel and matches the source doc's shorter form.
- **generateMetadata per page with locale-aware titles:** Meta reviewers and search engines see localized titles ("Política de Privacidad — MORFX" vs "Privacy Policy — MORFX") — demonstrates functional i18n for the verification team.

## Deviations from Plan

### Auto-fixed during execution

**1. [Rule 3 - Blocking] LegalSection component did not support recursive subsections (required for Privacy 7.2 structure)**

- **Found during:** Task 4 (Privacy ES transcription)
- **Issue:** Task 1's LegalSection treated `subsections[]` as a flat list of `LegalSubsection` objects without a `subsections` field. Privacy section 7.2 in source has two distinct bullet lists (data categories + processing purposes) with an intro paragraph between them. Rendering the second list required either (a) inflating 7.2 into two flat subsections (making section7.subsections.length = 9 and violating plan verification `length=8`) or (b) recursive subsections.
- **Fix:** Extended `LegalSubsection` interface with optional self-referencing `subsections?: LegalSubsection[]` field. Added recursive `Subsection` component (level parameter: 0 -> h3, 1+ -> h4 with left-border indent). Same change unblocks Privacy 7.6 (subencargados bullets + authorization paragraph) and 7.7 (ARCO bullets + canal y plazos paragraph) with the same pattern.
- **Files modified:** `src/components/marketing/legal/legal-section.tsx`
- **Verification:** Privacy JSON validates; section7.subsections.length === 8; `npx tsc --noEmit` clean.
- **Committed in:** b16e27f (bundled with Task 4 content commit; non-breaking extension to Task 1's component — additive optional field)
