**Part A: Fix somnio-agent.ts — Stop adding orchestrator templates to newTemplatesEnviados**

The current bug (line ~467-469 of somnio-agent.ts):
```typescript
const newTemplatesEnviados = orchestratorResult.stateUpdates?.templatesSent
```

This adds ALL templates that the orchestrator selected, regardless of whether they were actually sent. If a send is interrupted, templates that were never sent are permanently marked as sent — making Level 1 filter them out forever.
